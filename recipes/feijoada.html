Ingredients

  • 500g of black beans
  • 2 pepperoni sausages (cut into cubes)
  • 02 cans of creme de leite Nestle
  • 500g of dried meat
  • 1 big onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Green pepper flakes
  • Coriander (chopped)
  • Parsley
  • smell-green
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Soak the beans for at least one hour. Boil
    the dried meat twice, to desalt it..
  2. In the pressure cooker, place the onion,
    and the pepperoni sausage, so that the
    onion browns with the oil released by them.
    Once boiled, add the salted meat. Add the
    garlic and saut√© everything until the meat get
    more color. Add the washed beans, the large
    green pepper flakes and a pinch of black
    pepper.
  3. When the beans are cooked, collect the
    green pepper flakes from the pan and add
    the parsley, parsley, cilantro and powdered
    bean seasoning.
  4. If necessary, which it almost never is,
    correct the salt by adding whatever amount
    you think is necessary. While the beans are
    under pressure, prepare the farofa and
    cabbage.

Ingredients

  • 500 g or 01 pound of Lasagna pasta (of your choice)
  • 500 g or 01 pound of ground beef
  • 02 cans of creme de leite Nestle
  • 03 spoons of butter
  • 03 spoons of wheat flour
  • Homemade marina or store-tomato sauce
  • 500 g or 01 pound of ham
  • Salt to taste
  • 02 glasses of milk
  • 01 grated onion
  • 03 spoons of oil
  • 03 crushed garlic
  • 01 package of grated cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the manufacturer's
    instructions, pour it into a refractory with ice water
    to prevent sticking and set aside.
  2. Sauté the garlic, onion, ground beef, tomato
    sauce,let it cook for 3 minutes and set aside.
  3. Melt the butter, add the 3 tablespoons of wheat flour
    and stir.
  4. Pour the milk little by little and keep stirring.
  5. Lastly, add the cream, stir for 1 minute and turn off
    the heat.
  6. Pour one part of the bolognese sauce into a
    refractory, half the pasta, half the ham, half the
    >mozzarella, all the white sauce and the rest of the
    pasta.
  7. Repeat the layers to the edge of the container.
  8. Finish with the grated cheese and bake in a high oven
    (220°C or 428˚F), preheated, for about 20 minutes.

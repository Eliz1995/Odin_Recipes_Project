Brazilian
Strognoff

That is a classic strognoff recipe.
The bra zil ian recipe !!!

Ingredients

  • 02 boneless, skinless, chicken breasts (cut into very small pieces)
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 03 garlic cloves (minced)
  • ½ onion (diced)
  • 03 tablespoon ketchup
  • 01 tablespoon mustard
  • 01 cup heavy cream (or one can of table cream)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 4oz can sliced mushrooms (optional)
  • ¼ cup tomato sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt over the chicken and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt 1 tablespoon of butter over
    medium-high heat and add the olive oil; wait until it's
    hot and add chicken in a single layer. Don't disturb it
    until it starts to sear. Push it all to one side when
    pieces get some color and add 1 tablespoon of
    butter, onion, and garlic to the empty side. Saute
    onion and garlic until onion is translucent..
  3. Add heavy cream, ketchup, mustard, soy sauce,
    and canned mushrooms (if using). Mix everything..
  4. Check if the chicken is done; if it's not, lower the
    heat, cover the pan, and, let it cook for a bit longer
    (until no longer pink inside, or thermometer registers
    165ºF or 73˚C)
  5. Once the chicken is cooked but still soft and moist
    (don't over-cook it, or it will be hard and dry), turn off
    the heat and try it. Correct salt if necessary, and if
    you like it more acidic, add ¼ cup of tomato sauce..
  6. Enjoy it over rice, along with some potato sticks
    (Batata Palha).
